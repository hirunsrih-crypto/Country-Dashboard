Investment Thesis Summary

Recommendation: SLIGHTLY OVERWEIGHT — Composite score +0.64 falls in the Slightly Overweight band (+0.5 to +1.5).

Bull Case: 7 of 8 factors score positively. Macro fundamentals are strong (GDP 7.8%, CPI controlled at 4.2%, PMI expansionary). RBI's easing cycle provides tailwind. Brent crude at $73 is a sweet spot for India. Valuation premium vs EM at a 4-year low (10%) suggests the de-rating is largely done.

Bear Case: FII outflows remain persistent (-$601mn latest, negative for 8 of last 12 months). INR depreciation to 91 creates headwind for USD-denominated returns. Mirae India Midcap Fund underperforming Nifty and EM since H2 2024, reflecting midcap de-rating.

Key Risk: FII flow reversal is the swing factor — carries 18% weight and is the only negative score. A shift from outflows to inflows would push composite to ~1.0 (stronger overweight).
